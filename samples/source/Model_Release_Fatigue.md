Three new model releases dropped this week across different labs, and I barely registered them. A year ago each one felt like an event; now it is background noise. The benchmark numbers go up, the prices come down, and the practical delta between top models has narrowed enough that switching costs often outweigh capability gains.

What actually matters now is not which model is fractionally smarter but which ecosystem integrates cleanest into a given workflow. Anthropic is winning me over on the developer tooling side; Google on multimodal breadth and storage integration. OpenAI still has the widest third-party plugin surface. The model wars are quietly becoming a platform war, and that is a much slower and messier competition to watch.
