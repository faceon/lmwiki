I recently activated the 3-month free trial for Gemini Pro and was struck by Google's incredibly generous consumer policies, as they even canceled my existing subscription to apply the benefit. This consumer-first approach contrasts with past experiences where GPT's similar promotions excluded existing subscribers.

While OpenAI continues its breakneck pace in model capabilities, Google's ecosystem strategy—bundling tools like NotebookLM and Veo3 with 2TB of storage—feels like an overwhelming volume offensive. The stakes are astronomical; the total addressable market (TAM) for AI replacing wage labor is estimated at an unfathomable 80,000 trillion won. The ultimate winner of this AI war remains completely unpredictable.
